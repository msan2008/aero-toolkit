Notes:
Tubercle_amplitude: defined by height of tubercle feature relative to wing profile
Tubercle_wavelength: spacing/frequency of the tubercles along the leading edge
Tubercle_shape: category labels such as rectangle, sinusoidal_pointed, sinusoidal_curvy
root_chord: wing chord length at the root
tip_chord: wing chord length at the tip
sweep_angle: sweep angle of the wing
angle_of_attack: flow angle relative to the wing
airspeed: incoming flow speed
separation_x_over_c: separation location as a fraction of chord length
